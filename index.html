Coding

I first got into code tinkering as a kid when I realized I could make my very own MS Word clone in Visual Basic. I called it Maxisoft Type, created a proprietary extension, and quit Visual Basic in favor of C and PHP a couple weeks later.
Ten years later, I've opened a small school that teaches introductory courses on web development to students, hobbyists, and the occasional hipster. I love to share what I've learned over the years so I've started my own blog, covering everything from Laravel and jQuery to team management and dealing with stress.
